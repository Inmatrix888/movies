The Color Purple

The color purple/Film synopsis

An epic tale spanning forty years in the life of Celie (Whoopie Goldburg), an African-American woman living in the South who survives incredible abuse and bigotry"

After Celie's abusive father marries her off to the equally debasing "Mister" Albert Johnson (Danny Glover), things go from bad to worse,

leaving Celie to find companionship anywhere she can. She perseveres, holding on to her dream of one day being reunited with her sister in Africa. Based on a novel by Alice Walker.
